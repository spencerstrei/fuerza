Do I need an account?

No. Fuerza has no accounts and no sign-in. Open the app and start logging — everything is stored on your iPhone.

Where is my data stored? Is it backed up?

All of your workouts, routines, notes, and settings live on your device. Delete the app and the data goes with it, so export a full backup first from Settings → Export if you ever switch phones or reinstall.

How do I add my own routine?

Paste a program from notes, a coach, or any AI into Plan → Import and Fuerza builds the whole routine for you. Or start with the ready-made 4-Day Strength Split from onboarding.

How does the coach decide my targets?

Every target is computed on your device from your own logged history — beat last time, set by set, within your plan's rep ranges. Nothing is sent to a server. Flag a hard set or pain in your notes and the coach eases off.

The rest timer or notifications aren't firing

The rest timer uses notifications. Make sure notifications are allowed in iOS Settings → Fuerza → Notifications. The timer keeps running with your phone locked.

Music controls (Apple Music / Spotify)

Fuerza shows and controls your own playback during a workout. Apple Music asks for media-library permission; Spotify controls require the Spotify app installed on your phone. Both are optional and off until you use them.

Can I track machines, holds, or cardio?

Yes — machine-stack levels, timed holds (carries, planks) in seconds, and cardio by distance and pace, all alongside regular weight × reps. Switch units between lb and kg in Settings.
