Common questions

How do I import a routine or my history?
During onboarding (or from the Plan and History tabs) paste your routine or past workouts as plain text and Fuerza's on-device AI builds your plan and logs your history.

Is my data private?
Yes. Everything is stored on your device and the AI runs locally. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I add a custom exercise?
Open the Exercises tab and tap the + button. You can also auto-fill the muscles and cues with AI.
