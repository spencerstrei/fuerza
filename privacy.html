Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026
Fuerza collects nothing. Your training data lives on your iPhone and nowhere else.

What we collect

Nothing. Fuerza has no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising, and runs no servers of its own. All workouts, routines, notes, and settings are stored only on your device.

What stays on your device

  • Your workouts, exercises, routines, schedules, and notes
  • Your settings (units, rest timer, the name you choose to be greeted by)
  • All coaching features (targets, suggestions, insights) are computed entirely on your device. When on-device Apple Intelligence is used, your data is processed by Apple's on-device models and is never sent to us.

Optional integrations (all off until you use them)

  • Apple Music: used only to show and control your own playback during a workout. Requires your permission.
  • Spotify: connects to the Spotify app on your phone to show and control your own playback. Your Spotify credentials are handled by Spotify, not us.
  • Album artwork: while the Now Playing bar is shown and cover art isn't already on your device, the current track and artist names are sent to Apple's public iTunes catalog to fetch the artwork image. No account is involved and none of your training data is sent.
  • Calendar: if you turn on "Add workouts to Calendar," Fuerza writes your upcoming scheduled routine days into a dedicated "Workouts" calendar on your device. Those events live in whatever calendars your iPhone syncs (Apple, Google, Outlook) under your own account; turning it off removes them.
  • Notifications: used only for the optional rest timer, with your permission.

Sharing and export

Your training data (workouts, routines, notes, and settings) only leaves your device when you explicitly export or share it, for example sharing a workout summary or exporting your plan for an external AI. Where it goes is then entirely your choice. The optional integrations above are the only other times the app reaches the network, and none of them transmit your training data.

Data deletion

Delete the app and all data is deleted with it. You can export a full backup first from Settings.

Children

Fuerza does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if optional sync features are added) the update will appear here with a new effective date.
