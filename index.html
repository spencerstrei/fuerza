01Made for real training

Log a set in a single tap.

Every interaction is designed for one hand, mid-set: fast, quiet, and out of your way.

Hold & slide to adjust

Press any weight or rep and slide. No keyboard, no fiddling. Sets pre-fill from last session, so most of the time you just confirm.

Timers that ride in your pocket

A stopwatch since your last set, an optional rest countdown with locked-phone alerts, and a Log Set button right on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Your music rides along

Apple Music or Spotify, controlled inside your workout (album art, skips, scrubbing) without ever leaving the app.

ONE-TIME EDITS

Life-proof planning

Swap an exercise mid-workout, edit a single day, move a session, roll a skipped lift into your next one. None of it ever touches your actual plan.

02The coach

It knows your numbers, so you don't have to.

Every exercise opens with a concrete target computed from your own history: beat last time, set by set. Clear your rep range and it tells you to add weight. Stall for three sessions and it prescribes a smart back-off instead of letting you grind. Note pain and it never adds load.

03In the session

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

One card per exercise: your target, your sets, your notes, written inline like a text message, and read by the coach for next time.

  • Targets in your exercise's own language: lb, kg, machine levels, or seconds held
  • PR detection with a gold landmark the moment you earn it
  • Quick-log a whole session in plain text: “bench 185 8,8,7”
04The receipts

Progress, honestly counted.

A deterministic coach's read, weekly volume against your own average, workouts against your plan, PRs, streaks, and an activity calendar. Every number computed from your logs, never invented.

  • “So far this week” honesty on partial weeks
  • A plan line on the frequency chart: are you hitting it?
  • Rearrange or hide every section. Your dashboard.
05Your plan, your way

Paste a program. Get a plan.

Bring a program from your notes, your coach, or your favorite AI, and Fuerza turns it into a fully configured weekly plan. No program yet? Start with the ready-made 4-day split, or let us hand your AI a coach prompt that writes one for you.

  • Import years of history from any app or spreadsheet
  • Schedule across your week, reschedule one-off days
  • Export everything, anytime. It's your data.
