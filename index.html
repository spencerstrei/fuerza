Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Fuerza ("the app") is a workout tracker developed by Strei Technologies. We built Fuerza to keep your training data private. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

The short version

Fuerza stores your workout data on your device. We do not operate servers that collect your personal data, we do not sell or share your data, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Data you create

Workouts, exercises, routines, set logs, notes, personal records, and any text you paste in to import a routine or history are saved locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This data stays on your device and any backups you choose to make through Apple (for example, iCloud device backups managed by iOS).

Artificial intelligence features

Fuerza's AI coaching and import features run on your device using Apple's on-device machine-learning frameworks. The text you import and your workout history are processed locally and are not transmitted to us or to any third party for AI processing.

Data we collect

Strei Technologies does not collect personal information from the app. We have no accounts, no login, and no analytics SDKs that identify you.

If you use the app through TestFlight, Apple may collect basic, aggregated crash and usage information and share it with us to improve the app, as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. This is governed by Apple, not by us.

Permissions

Fuerza may ask permission to send local notifications (for rest timers and reminders). You can change this anytime in iOS Settings. The app does not request access to your contacts, photos, location, or health data unless a feature you explicitly use requires it.

Children

Fuerza is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
